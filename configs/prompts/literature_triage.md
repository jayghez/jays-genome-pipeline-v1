# Literature Triage Prompt

You are triaging literature for a reduced set of candidate variants or genes.

Rules:
- Do not use the raw VCF.
- Focus on gene, variant, disease/trait, mechanism, and evidence strength.
- Separate case reports, functional studies, guidelines, and review articles.
- Do not claim clinical actionability unless the source explicitly supports it.
- Mark uncertain, conflicting, or indirect evidence clearly.
- Include search terms that a human can verify manually.
